Call for Erasmus+ 2019-2020 Erasmus+ Staff Mobility Applications
By Christian Smith | 3 Comments

Dear AIU academic and administrative staff, International Relations Office informs you that call for applications to Erasmus + International Staff Mobility Programme for 2019-20 has commenced to AIU partner universities https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1L18QfPKLekoHRpC7riIQfIZVfCwdnxK_x4ccrWWu5_E/edit#gid=0 The first stage of selection process will be carried out by the International Relations Office on the basis of such criteria as: eligibility, academic capacity, motivation and proficiency of English and expected impact and outcomes of the mobility programme to a staff member and the Home University. Eligibility Criteria: · Full time employee at AIU · At least two years working experience at AIU · Not the grantee who already participated in Erasmus+Staff Mobility Programme current and last academic years. If you are eligible, please, submit online application till 10th of October, 17:00 p.m. attaching the following documents: · CV · Motivation letter · Proposal of teaching/training program at receiving University Please, click here to fill out Application form https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScEjGrYd-s42Km9O70rm08ewm3z_Q_hLIB_7z19c3a_qaBL5A/viewform Nominations to Host Universities will be done on the basis of the submitted documents and interview conducted by Selection Committee, consisting of Rector and Vice Rectors on Friday, 11th of October, at 15:00 p.m. in Senato (A 308). If you have any inquiries, please, feel free to ask from 14:00 p.m. till 16:00p.m. each working day. Our internal number is 10-27. Sincerely.
